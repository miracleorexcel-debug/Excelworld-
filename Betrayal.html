Betrayal

Home Love Broken Regret Betrayal

  1. We ate together,
    You smiled at me,
    Yet you poisoned me.
    And you called that love.

  2. The time we shared was gold, Yet feels like nothing ever existed. Am I still in a dream? How heartless of you. 💔

  3. You called me your own,
    But you stabbed me in my back.
    Is this really love?
    Or just a beautiful lie? 💔

  4. You said you loved me,
    That I was your sugar.
    Now you wander around freely.
    Am I now bitter?

  5. I walked a thousand paths, never looking back,
    with a solitary heart.
    Alone along the way,
    yet searching for a friend and a partner.
    I looked back.
    Even at your worst, I picked you up.
    The road looked clear.
    But you bit me —
    and fed my flesh to the very dogs I bled to protect you from.
    It hurts because I once loved you the most.
    But the lesson was worth it, because it saved me from a thousand more bites.
    But it still hurts.
